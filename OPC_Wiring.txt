OPC GND - Black  -> power supply GND and WisBlock GND
OPC 5V+ - Red    -> separate 5V power supply
OPC SS  - Green  -> WisBlock breakout CS
OPC SDI - Blue   -> WisBlock breakout MOSI/SDI
OPC SDO - White  -> WisBlock breakout MISO/SDO
OPC SCK - Yellow -> WisBlock breakout SCK

GND - Black
5V+ - Red
SS  - Green
SDI - Blue
SDO - White
SCK - Yellow
